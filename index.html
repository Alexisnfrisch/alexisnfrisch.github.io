RESUME
Alexis Frisch
UX/UI Design | Salesforce Development

Currently working as a Product Design Specialist for SimplyHome, I have crafted a position that thrives at the intersection of creating intentional focus toward UX/UI Design and CRM development.

I have had the opportunity to craft myself into the 'resident Salesforce subject matter expert' during a time of transition from two systems into the singular Salesforce platform. During this transition, I've learned how to restructure our object hierarchy, work cross-departmentally to ensure optimal system buy-in and performance through building workflows and layouts, build custom reports and dashboards, provide company training and support for all process updates and releases, manage user permissions, effectively use Dataloader.io, and even light Apex development along the way.

Through creating mocks and wireframes for our organization, I've also been able to focus on maximizing usability and crafting the user experience through our system's web portal. I've always had a passion for film and design, and attending a vocational highschool for Digital Video Production afforded me the opportunity to pursue those passions from a young age. My work in Hospitality and Rental Management grew my appreciation for the consumer, and my constant need to incorporate creative aspects into everyday duties made me realize design would always be a passion.

I'm recreationally passionate about special effects makeup and animatronic creatures, my wife and dogs, Xbox gaming, and all things Halloween.Practical Effects > CGI effects. In my free time, I'm learning how to crochet and listening to an audiobook, fixing something around the house based on a Youtube tutorial, or playing the piano. I love gyros, pad thai, and bbq chicken pizza.
